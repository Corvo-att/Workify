**1\. Public & Discovery Pages (The "Guest" Experience)**

These pages are for SEO and converting visitors into registered users.

* **Landing Page (Home):** Hero section with two CTAs ("Hire Talent" or "Find Work"), trending skill categories, and trust signals (testimonials/stats).  
* **Browse Jobs:** A searchable, filterable list of active postings. Filters should include budget (fixed vs. hourly), experience level, and category.  
* **Browse Talent:** A directory of freelancer profiles, allowing clients to "scout" before posting a job.  
* **How it Works:** Separate sections explaining the workflow for both freelancers (Bid \-\> Work \-\> Get Paid) and clients (Post \-\> Hire \-\> Pay).

**2\. Authentication & Onboarding**

Because users have different roles, onboarding is more complex than a simple login.

* **Login/Register**  
* **Role Selection:** A screen asking, "I want to hire for a project" vs. "I’m looking for work."  
* **Freelancer Profile Setup:** A multi-step form to input skills, hourly rate, portfolio links, and professional bio.  
* **Client Company Profile:** A page for company details, industry, and payment method verification.

**3\. The Freelancer Workspace**

This is where the user manages their career.

* **Freelancer Dashboard:** A snapshot of active proposals, invitations to interview, and current earnings.  
* **Job Details Page:** The expanded view of a job post. It should show the client's history (rating, total spent) and an "Apply Now" button.  
* **Proposal Submission Page:** A form for the freelancer to write a cover letter, set their bid amount, and propose a timeline/milestones.  
* **My Proposals:** A list tracking "Submitted," "Active," and "Archived" bids.  
* **Active Contracts:** A management view for ongoing work, where they can submit milestones for review.

**4\. The Client Workspace**

This is where the user manages their projects and spending.

* **Client Dashboard:** Overview of active job postings, new applicants to review, and total spend.  
* **Post a Job (Wizard):** A multi-step form:  
  1. **Title & Category**  
  2. **Description & Skills**  
  3. **Budget (Fixed/Hourly) & Duration**  
* **Review Proposals:** A list of all freelancers who applied to a specific job. Include a "Shortlist" or "Decline" feature.  
* **Hire/Offer Page:** Where the client defines the final contract terms and deposits the first milestone into escrow.

**5\. Collaboration & Finance (Shared Pages)**

These pages look similar for both roles but show different data.

* **Messages/Chat Room:** A real-time interface for interviewing and project updates.  
* **Contract Management Page:** The "source of truth" for a specific hire. It tracks the status of milestones (Funded, In Review, Released).  
* **The Wallet/Transaction History:** A ledger showing all deposits, withdrawals, and pending payments.  
* **Dispute Center:** A basic form to "Flag a Project" if work isn't delivered or payment is withheld.

**6\. Admin Panel (Internal)**

Since you are the platform owner, you need a way to manage the system.

* **User Management:** Ability to ban users or verify identities.  
* **Job Moderation:** Reviewing posts for spam or "off-platform" payment requests.  
* **Financial Overview:** Monitoring the total "Escrow" balance and platform fees collected.
